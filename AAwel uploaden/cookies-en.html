Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website.

2. What cookies and storage do we use?

  • Necessary cookies - essential for the website to work (e.g. session, shopping cart, payment)
  • Local storage - used for language preference and consent status
  • Technical third-party cookies - such as Cloudflare and PayPal when needed for security or payment

3. How do you manage cookies?

You can manage your choice through the cookie banner. Your consent choice is stored in localStorage. You can change it later by clearing site data or browser storage. Some features may not work properly if necessary cookies are blocked.

4. Third parties

We use Cloudflare for hosting and security, which may place technical cookies. PayPal places cookies during the payment process. Please consult their own privacy policies for more information.

5. Consent

On your first visit, we ask for your consent to use non-essential cookies where applicable. You can withdraw or change your choice at any time by clearing site data or browser storage.
