🔥 How to apply the patch
1 Place the patch on the desired spot.
2 Cover with a thin cloth or baking paper.
3 Iron firmly for 20-30 seconds without steam.
4 Let cool and check the adhesion.
5 For heavy use, we recommend sewing the patch on extra securely.
